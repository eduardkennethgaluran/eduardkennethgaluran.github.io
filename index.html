About Us

ChuYameAnne History

ChuYameAnne Online Bakeshop is one of the small business here in the Philippines and it was DTI approved for online business. It was established on July 2019. Since, online business become popular in the Philippines, ChuYameAnne Online Bakeshop decided to build their own small business. Ube pandesal was the first product they made for their business. After the day passed, they decided to create a customized cakes and cupcakes for occasions such as birthday, wedding, baptism, etc. ChuYameAnne Online Bakeshop also make baked sushi, ice creams, choco butternut loaf and munchkins and many more. It is a home-based business and do not have physical store.
